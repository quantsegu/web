Glossary & disclaimer

All costs are estimates based on publicly available data and market research as of 2025. School fees change annually — verify directly with institutions. PISA data: OECD PISA 2022. University rankings: QS World University Rankings 2025. Cost of living: Numbeo 2025, Mercer Cost of Living 2025. This tool is for indicative planning purposes only and does not constitute financial or educational advice. Exchange rates are approximate (EUR reference rates).

Country-specific notes
  • NL: Kinderopvangtoeslag can offset 70–96% of eligible childcare for working parents.
  • CH: ETH #7 globally at CHF 730/yr tuition. Pre-school childcare is the largest family cost.
  • DE: Public universities charge zero tuition to all students including international.
  • SG: Foreign children need MOE ballot for public school — international school near-mandatory for expats.
  • UAE: No free public education for expatriates. Check KHDA ratings before enrolling.
  • LU: Among highest child benefits in Europe — offsets school costs for residents.
