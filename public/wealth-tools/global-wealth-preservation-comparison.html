Methodology & disclaimer

Scope: High-level planning model for private individuals. It does not replace tax, legal, immigration, or investment advice. Cantonal, municipal, treaty, entity-structure, and product-specific rules are simplified.

Net worth = liquid portfolio (after estimated annual tax drag) + pension capital + property equity. Salary converts to savings via your rate or fixed override until retirement.

Jurisdiction highlights: Switzerland — private movable capital gains generally untaxed; wealth tax placeholder. Netherlands — Box 3–style wealth drag. Germany — ~26.375% on financial investment gains/dividends (saver allowance not fully modelled). Belgium — 2026+ capital-gains reform on financial assets at planning level; pre-2026 low-friction assumption phased out. UAE — no personal income tax on wages/investment/rental under current individual assumptions; corporate/business activity may differ. Singapore — no capital gains tax generally; property friction included. Luxembourg — capital gains on portfolio subject to holding-period / substantial-participation rules at planning level.

Relocation: The seven columns each assume full residence in that jurisdiction for the entire horizon. Enabling “Model move” adds a dashed Your move path: start in your base jurisdiction, switch at the move year — useful for stress-testing one timeline, not a simultaneous residency ranking.

Currency: Internal amounts in CHF; EUR and USD views use your FX inputs.
